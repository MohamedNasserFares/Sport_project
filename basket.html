Introduction

Basketball is a popular team sport in which two teams of five players each compete. They both try to get the ball into the opponent's basket,score goals and earn points. Points are scored by inserting the ball into the 3-meter basket; The team that scores more points than its opponent at the end of the match wins, so it is more difficult for both teams to score. The player can move the ball forward by bouncing it on the field in what is known as (dribbling) or passing it to his teammates to reach the goal. No physical contact is allowed to obstruct a player (foul) and there are restrictions on how the ball is handled known as (the rules of play).

Basketball history

First basketball court: Springfield College. In early December 1891, Canadian Dr. James Naismith, Professor of Physical Education at McGill University in Montreal, who also worked as an instructor at the YMCA Training School (now Springfield College in Springfield, Massachusetts, USA) conducted research on creating a strong indoor game venue that His students occupy and maintain their physical fitness at appropriate levels in the long winters that prevail in the states located in the New England region, and this was the game of basketball.
