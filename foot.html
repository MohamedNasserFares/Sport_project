Introduction

Football is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players each playing with a ball. Football is played by 250 million players in more than two hundred countries around the world, so it is the most popular and widespread sport in the world. Football is played on a rectangular court with two goals in either side. The aim of the game is to score goals by kicking the ball into the goal.

Etymology

There are conflicting interpretations of the origin of the word "football". It is widely assumed that the word "soccer" (or the phrase "soccer") refers to the act of kicking a ball with the foot. An alternative explanation is that soccer originally referred to a variety of games in medieval Europe that were played on foot. There is no conclusive evidence for either interpretation.
